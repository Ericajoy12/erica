ABOUT MATU CAFÉ HUB

MATU CAFÉ HUB Offers a unique coffee house environment unlike any other.

We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (although you are more than welcome to do that),

we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that joy-made cup of coffee.

If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you.

If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone fire place that is perfect for your weary mind and body.

We look forward to serving you at MATU CAFÉ HUB!
